CALCULATOR WEB APP STRATEGY FOR GOOGLE ADS INCOME
===================================================

KEY DECISION: ONE SITE, MULTIPLE CATEGORIES
--------------------------------------------
- Keep everything on one domain. Domain authority compounds.
- Every backlink, indexed page, and month of age strengthens the entire site.
- Cross-niche internal linking keeps users on site longer (more pageviews = more ads).
- One codebase, one hosting bill, one analytics dashboard.

SITE STRUCTURE (subdirectories, not separate sites)
----------------------------------------------------
calculators.com/
  /finance/mortgage-calculator
  /finance/compound-interest-calculator
  /construction/concrete-calculator
  /construction/lumber-calculator
  /math/percentage-calculator
  /health/bmi-calculator
  /health/calorie-calculator

Each category gets its own index page acting as a hub.

WHY CALCULATORS ARE THE BEST ADSENSE PLAY
-------------------------------------------
- High CPC ads: users have commercial intent, Google serves premium ads.
- Evergreen: build once, earn for years.
- Multi-page sessions: users try 2-3 calculators per visit.
- SEO-friendly: each calculator targets a specific keyword.
- Programmatically scalable: template the UI, crank out pages.
- Low maintenance: no user accounts, no moderation, no content treadmill.
- Link-worthy: people naturally link to useful tools.

NICHE PRIORITY (by CPC x volume)
----------------------------------
1. Finance      - mortgage, loan, compound interest, retirement ($5-50+ CPC)
2. Tax          - income tax, capital gains, sales tax ($5-30 CPC)
3. Real Estate  - affordability, rent vs buy, closing costs ($5-20 CPC)
4. Insurance    - life insurance needs, car insurance ($10-40 CPC)
5. Construction - concrete, lumber, roofing, paint ($3-10 CPC, underserved)
6. Business     - profit margin, break-even, markup ($3-15 CPC)
7. Health       - BMI, calorie, macro, due date ($2-10 CPC)
8. Math         - percentage, fraction (low CPC but massive volume for authority)

LAUNCH ORDER
-------------
Phase 1: 10-15 finance calculators with solid SEO content.
Phase 2: Add supporting blog posts targeting long-tail keywords.
Phase 3: Expand to construction, tax, business, health categories.
Phase 4: Layer in affiliate offers alongside AdSense for 5-10x revenue boost.
